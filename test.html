ICT Seminar 2025 - Seoul

Exploring Innovations in ICT for a Smarter Future

About the Event

The ICT Seminar 2025, hosted in Seoul, brings together industry leaders, researchers, and students to discuss the transformative role of Information and Communication Technology. This event will showcase the latest innovations, explore trends in AI, cloud computing, and 5G, and provide a platform for networking and collaboration.
