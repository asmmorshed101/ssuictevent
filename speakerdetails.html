Dr. Jeong won Yoon

AWS Korea CEO

Dr. Yoon is the Director of the Public Sector at AWS Korea, spearheading digital innovation and cloud adoption across Korea’s public sector. He holds a B.S. and M.S. in Computer Engineering from California State University, Sacramento, and a Ph.D. in Information Management from Seoul Venture University. Previously, he served as Director of the Global Division at the National Information Society Agency (NIA) and as Telecommunications Coordinator at the UN International Y2K Cooperation Center. He was also a Visiting Researcher at the U.S. National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST), gaining experience in global ICT standards and collaboration. He is a recognized leader with extensive experience in driving digital transformation and cloud policy for the public sector.
